When making a character, you may choose from one of these elements to be your Toon's type.

Fire- 30% chance to Burn enemy.
Water- Heal all allies by 3 health every 3 turns.
Earth- 10% chance to Stun enemy if Earth attack is used.
Wind- Damage is reduced by 20%.
Electric- 40% chance to Stun robotic enemies, and 15% chance for all other enemies.
