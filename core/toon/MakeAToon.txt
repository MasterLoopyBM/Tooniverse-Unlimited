Species list:
Dog- All Dogs start with 4 extra health.
Cat- All Cats start with 10 extra magic.
Mouse- All Mice start with 3 extra health and can reduce enemy attacks by 20%.
Rabbit- All Rabbits start with 12 extra magic and can reduce enemy magic attacks by 15%.
Duck- Ducks can ignore Physical limitation in Underwater terrain.
Horse- All Horses start with 5 extra health and can increase allies' attack by 10%.
Monkey- All Monkeys start with 10 extra magic and can increase allies' attack by 10%.
Pig- Pigs can ignore Physical limitation in Swamp terrain.
Bear- Bears start with 5 extra health and can ignore Poison and Stun effects.
